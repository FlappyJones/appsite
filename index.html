Guiding a levitating head is not as easy as you think!

Flappy Jones is a side-scrolling mobile game for Android devices based on the hit game Flappy Birds. The objective is to direct a flying human head, named "Mr. Jones", between stacks of desktop computers. If "Mr. Jones" touches a computer, the game is over. "Mr. Jones" floats upwards every time the player taps the screen. However, if the player does not touch the screen, "Mr. Jones" falls. Each pair of desktops that he navigates between earns the player a point.
